India's rural healthcare crisis affects 65% of the population (900M+ people) with only 25% of resources, causing:

Delayed treatment from lack of immediate medical guidance

Specialist doctor shortages at Primary Health Centers (PHCs)

Language barriers blocking effective communication

Poor emergency response systems

Lost paper-based medical records

Preventable complications from minor treatable conditions

MediConnect Rural delivers AI symptom analysis, emergency SOS, digital health records, and doctor connectivity via basic smartphones to save lives in underserved villages.
